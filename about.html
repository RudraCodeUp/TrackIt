What is TrackIt?

TrackIt is a simple but powerful habit tracking application designed to help you build consistent routines and achieve your goals. It works entirely in your browser and saves all data locally on your device.

How to Use TrackIt

  • 1

    Create Habits

  • 2

    Track Daily Progress

    Check off your habits as you complete them each day.

  • 3

    Review Your Analytics

  • 4

    Stay Consistent

    Build streaks and achieve long-term habit formation.

Habit Building Tips

Start Small

Begin with easy habits that take less than two minutes to complete.

Stack Habits

Attach new habits to existing routines to make them easier to remember.

Environment Matters

Design your environment to make good habits obvious and easy.

Never Miss Twice

If you miss one day, try not to miss the next one to maintain momentum.
