About TrackIt

Welcome to TrackIt!

An offline habit & study tracker designed to help you stay consistent and visualize your progress.

How to Use

  1. Go to Add Habits and create one.
  2. Return to Dashboard to mark progress.
  3. Check your charts in Analytics.
  4. Customize appearance in Settings.

Features

  • Offline Storage
  • Theme Toggle
  • Charts & Streaks
  • No login needed
